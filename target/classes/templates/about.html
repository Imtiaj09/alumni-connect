About Alumni Connect

School-first alumni management platform for verified community, memories, and impact.

Our Mission

Alumni Connect is a cloud-based SaaS platform that modernizes alumni operations for schools in Bangladesh. It connects software owner, school admin, batch controller (CR), and alumni members in one secure workflow.

The goal is long-term institutional connection: notices, events, gallery memories, mentoring, and donation impact with role-based access and moderation.

Core Roles

Software Owner

Add schools, assign school admins, control platform-level features and monitor analytics.

School Admin

Manage members, create batches, assign CR permissions, approve content, and run school campaigns.

Member & CR

Build alumni network, join events, upload gallery images, post updates, and support fund goals.

Why It Works

School-Isolated Data

Each school data remains isolated with strict access boundaries.

Role-Based Permission

Admins can grant and revoke CR capabilities with transparent control.

Verified Alumni

Verification and moderation keep community quality and trust high.

Impact Workflows

Events, posts, gallery, and donations run in one integrated workflow.
